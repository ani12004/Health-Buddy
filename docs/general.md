# Health Buddy AI - General Overview

Health Buddy AI is a modern, AI-powered health companion designed to provide instant symptom analysis, health insights, and a seamless connection between patients and doctors.

## Purpose
The primary goal of Health Buddy AI is to simplify healthcare management by using advanced AI models (Google Gemini) to help users understand their symptoms and maintain a direct line of communication with healthcare providers.

## Key Features

### For Patients
- **AI Symptom Checker**: Enter symptoms and receive an instant analysis of potential conditions and severity levels.
- **Appointment Management**: Schedule and track appointments with medical professionals.
- **Medication Tracking**: Keep a digital record of all active prescriptions and dosages.
- **AI Health Companion**: A dedicated chat interface for general health questions and advice.
- **Dashboard**: A personalized overview of upcoming health tasks and recent reports.

### For Doctors
- **Patient Management**: View detailed medical histories and reports for assigned patients.
- **Digital Prescriptions**: Create and manage prescriptions directly through the platform.
- **Dashboard**: A clinical overview of patient activity and appointment schedules.

## Core Values
- **Simplicity**: A clean, intuitive interface for both patients and doctors.
- **Accessibility**: Quick access to health insights without complex onboarding steps.
- **Privacy**: Secure authentication handled by Supabase, ensuring data is accessible only to authorized users.

## User Experience
The application features a premium, responsive design with support for both Light and Dark modes. It uses high-performance animations and a modern typography system (Manrope) to provide a professional and trustworthy clinical experience.
